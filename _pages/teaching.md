I teach courses that connect sociological theory and empirical research with questions about health, aging, social relationships, and inequality.

## Selected syllabi

These syllabi give a quick sense of the course aims, readings, and learning activities. Course plans are updated for each offering.

- [Health and Social Behavior (SOC4046), Hanyang University — Semester 1, 2026](/assets/pdf/teaching/Health_and_Social_Behavior_2026.pdf): medical sociology, health inequalities, stress, social isolation, loneliness, and health-care systems.
- [Analysis of Social Problems (SOC3032), Hanyang University — Semester 2, 2025](/assets/pdf/teaching/Analysis_of_Social_Problems_2025.pdf): inequality, population aging, environmental crises, crime, and the digital divide.
