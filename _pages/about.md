I am a sociologist. My research navigates three main themes:

1. Complexity and dynamics of social relationships in later life
2. Stress, coping, and mental well-being among family caregivers
3. Social capital in a comparative perspective

I teach Health and Social Behavior (SOC4046) and Social Networks and Social Capital (SOC9115) at [Hanyang University](https://socio.hanyang.ac.kr/home) (2025).

Previously, I was an Assistant Professor of Sociology at [Hong Kong Baptist University](https://socweb.hkbu.edu.hk/) (2023–2024) and a Research Fellow at the [Centre for Ageing Research and Education, Duke-NUS Medical School](https://www.duke-nus.edu.sg/care) (2020–2023). I received a Ph.D. in Sociology from the [National University of Singapore](https://fass.nus.edu.sg/socanth/) (2020).
